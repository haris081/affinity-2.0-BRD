Affinity Insurance 2.0 Platform

A comprehensive digital solution for managing affinity insurance programs

Platform Overview

Affinity 2.0 is a web-based, configurable platform that enables insurance brokers to sell tailored insurance products to specific groups or organizational members who share common characteristics or interests.

The platform provides end-to-end digital capabilities for partner onboarding, product configuration, policy issuance, claims management, and business analytics.

Key Features

Partner Onboarding & Management

Efficiently create and manage partner accounts with organizational structure configuration and distribution channel mapping.

Product Configuration

Create and customize insurance products with specific features, riders, and add-ons tailored to partner needs.

Premium Rule Engine

Define customized premium calculation rules based on factors like age, sum insured, family size, and industry type.

COI Issuance

Generate individual certificates in real-time through API integration or internal platform generation.

Bulk Upload Capabilities

Upload multiple member details at once using Excel or CSV files for efficient certificate issuance.

Claims Management

End-to-end claim processing with configurable templates, tracking, and analytics dashboard.

Endorsement Module

Enable policy modifications and cancellations with automated refund processing and audit trails.

Hierarchy Management

Role-based access control with data scoping and visibility settings for different user levels.

Customer Journey Types

Retail / Non-Assisted Journey

Self-service journey where customers access insurance through partner URLs, complete forms, view quotes, and make payments independently.

Sales / Assisted Journey

Sales staff initiate journeys on behalf of customers, create leads, collect details, and assist with the entire purchase process.

Embedded Journey

Seamless integration of insurance into partner platforms via API or redirection, offering insurance during relevant transactions.

User Roles & Responsibilities

Super Admin

Top-level user with full system configuration access, rule engine management, and master data control.

Broker Admin

Manages insurer, partner, and product configurations, monitors CD balances, and configures payment workflows.

Partner Admin

Manages customer journeys, tracks CD balances, monitors certificates, and configures notifications.

Sales Manager

Conducts purchase journeys on behalf of customers and monitors issued certificates.

Branch Operations

Reviews proposals and approves payments at the branch level.

Central Operations

Approves bulk certificate payments and validates central-level transactions.

Dashboard Capabilities

Insurer Dashboard

Centralized overview of platform performance, partner activity, premium collection, and business metrics.

Broker Dashboard

Sales tracking, client portfolio management, commission structure, and team performance analytics.

Partner Dashboard

Business performance insights, client activity tracking, policy management, and commission earnings.
